Expected Data Format

Your data should include features commonly found in exoplanet datasets:

  • Orbital Period - Time for one complete orbit
  • Transit Duration - Duration of the transit event
  • Planetary Radius - Radius of the planet
  • Stellar Radius - Radius of the host star
  • Impact Parameter - Transit impact parameter
  • Transit Depth - Depth of the transit
